　Hello！

　This is Craftman Story Vol. 0.This is a preparatory issue, so to speak, introducing our activities and members.It is also a statement of determination that this is what we are going to do from now on.

What is the Craftman Story?

　This is a project of Tokyo University of Science IDM focusing on engineers and craftsmen of Ota Ward town factories.The goal of this project is to promote the craftsmen of Ota Ward's factories to the world through student-led interviews, website articles, and video production.

Member Introduction

Web Team

Sakura Tokumaru

　I mainly do web production. This is my first web production for this site.
